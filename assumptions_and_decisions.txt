# Assumptions and Decisions
## Assumptions
- Assumes that the Guild would have the resources to assign staff members to moderate the platform
- Assumes that society representative users refer to the society itself, not an actual person, so that the individual may still have a student account, and the society account can be passed between committees.

## Decisions
- Decided to make event categories based around the UN Climate Goals
- Decided to make permissions restrict ability to reach certain pages to simplify security and permissions
